Submission Guidelines

Guidelines:

  • Use bold font “Times New Roman” size 12 for the title.
  • Name/s of author/s must be written as initials and surname centered after the Title.
  • Use font “Times New Roman” size 11 with 1.5 line spacing for the text.
  • The text should be between 300 and 600 words excluding references and acknowledgement.
  • Page layout should be A4 with 1-inch top, bottom, left, and right margins.
  • Keywords: maximum 5.

Keywords: Research, AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, Innovation

Acknowledgement:

Grant funding can be acknowledged at the end of the document. Any utilization of generative AI tools in the research or writing should be appropriately credited.
