About Peracom Postgraduate Colloquium

Introduction

The Peracom Postgraduate Colloquium (PPC) is an annual event organized to bring together postgraduate students, researchers, and professionals to present and discuss the latest advancements in various fields of engineering, technology, and innovation. The colloquium serves as a platform for knowledge exchange and collaboration, encouraging young researchers to showcase their work and engage with the academic community.

Objective

The primary objective of PPC is to foster an environment where postgraduate students can present their research to a broad audience, receive constructive feedback, and build networks with peers and industry professionals. By providing an opportunity for knowledge dissemination, the colloquium supports the growth and development of innovative ideas in engineering and technology.

Participants

The colloquium is open to postgraduate students pursuing master's and doctoral programs, as well as early-career researchers. Industry professionals and academics are also invited to participate as keynote speakers, panelists, or mentors, creating a dynamic environment for learning and networking.

Organizing Committee

The PPC organizing committee comprises a group of dedicated postgraduate students and faculty members from the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya. The committee works together to ensure the success of the event by planning and executing all aspects, including speaker invitations, paper reviews, and event logistics.

Event Highlights

  • Keynote addresses from prominent figures in academia and industry
  • Paper presentations by postgraduate students on a variety of research topics
  • Workshops and panel discussions on current trends and challenges in engineering
  • Opportunities for networking and collaboration
