Peracom Postgraduate Colloquium

Join us at the University of Peradeniya.

25th November 2024
University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka

Event Overview

The Department of Computer Engineering warmly invites you to the 1st Annual Doctoral Colloquium. This event offers graduate researchers a platform to showcase their work in a constructive atmosphere, allowing them to gain insights and feedback from a diverse audience of peers and academics.

Theme: "Technology for Sustainability"

This theme aligns with the various research areas and expertise within the Department of Computer Engineering.

Keynote Speaker

Vijaykrishnan Narayanan

Associate Dean of Innovation, College of Engineering

Evan Pugh University Professor and Robert A. Noll Chair Professor of Computer Science and Engineering and Electrical Engineering, The Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA 16802



Eligibility

All graduate researchers of the department are eligible and encouraged to submit to the postgraduate colloquium. We welcome research at all levels of progress and completion.
